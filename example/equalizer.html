Equalizer

Equalizer makes it dead simple to give multiple equal height.

Basics

To set up an Equalizer group, you need a container, which gets the attribute data-equalizer, and then a series of child elements, which get the attribute data-equalizer-watch. The child elements will all be resized to have the same height.

<div class="row" data-equalizer>
 <div class="column small-12 medium-4">
 <div class="section primary" data-equalizer-watch>
 <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/485">
 </div>
 </div>
 <div class="column small-12 medium-4">
 <div class="section primary" data-equalizer-watch>
 <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consetetur sadipscing elitr, sed diam nonumy eirmod tempor invidunt ut labore et
 dolore magna aliquyam erat, sed diam voluptua.</p>
 </div>
 </div>
 <div class="column small-12 medium-4">
 <div class="section primary" data-equalizer-watch>
 <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/485x248">
 </div>
 </div>
</div>
